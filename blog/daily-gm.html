Oneliq Portal is now live

Oneliq Portal is your daily on-ramp to Arc Testnet. Each check-in is a real transaction on Arc, verified on-chain before it counts toward your streak — a simple, repeatable way to get familiar with the network, now with onboarding tasks, badges, and referrals.

Oneliq Team · June 16, 2026 · 5 min read

Oneliq Portal is a daily check-in experience built on Arc Testnet. Each check-in is recorded as a real transaction on Arc and verified on-chain before it contributes to your streak.

The campaign is designed to make interacting with Arc simple and repeatable. Instead of asking people to learn a new network through tutorials, Oneliq Portal offers a lightweight way to build familiarity through consistent, everyday use — and now an easy way to bring friends along.

Why we built it

One of the biggest challenges for any new network is onboarding. Many users create a wallet, claim testnet assets, and stop there — the first meaningful interaction usually means understanding how the network works, finding an application to use, and completing a transaction.

Oneliq Portal was created to lower that barrier. The experience is intentionally simple: open the page, complete a daily check-in, and record activity on Arc Testnet. Over time, those repeated interactions help users grow comfortable with the network and its ecosystem.

How it works

You can complete one check-in per day. When you check in, your wallet signs a transaction on Arc Testnet; once that transaction is confirmed and verified, the check-in is counted and your streak is updated.

Every streak is therefore backed by actual on-chain activity rather than off-chain actions. The transaction stays visible on Arc and can be viewed through the network explorer like any other transaction.

Streaks and freezes

Oneliq Portal includes a streak system to encourage consistent participation: every successful daily check-in increases your streak count.

To keep an occasional missed day from resetting your progress, each account has a limited number of freeze credits. If you miss a day, an available freeze is used automatically and your streak continues. Additional freezes can be earned through milestones and future community activities.

Badges and milestones

You can unlock badges by taking part in the campaign and interacting with the broader Oneliq ecosystem. Progress toward milestone badges is shown directly in the interface.

  • Welcome to Oneliq
    Awarded after completing all five onboarding tasks. Earning it also grants the Early Oneliq role in Discord.
  • Connector
    Awarded after ten friends join and complete onboarding through your personal Portal referral link. A simple thank-you for helping more people find Arc.
  • 7-Day Streak
    Awarded after maintaining a streak for seven consecutive days.
  • 100 Transactions
    Awarded after reaching one hundred verified transactions on Arc Testnet.
  • OG
    Reserved for verified OG members of the Oneliq community.

This is the current line-up — additional badges will be introduced over time.

Invite your friends

Portal now includes a lightweight referral system. Every connected wallet gets a personal link with a short code — oneliq.xyz/portal?ref=<your-code> — that you can share anywhere. When someone opens Portal through your link and connects their wallet, they're counted as your invite.

There's no limit on how many people you can invite. Once ten of them join and finish onboarding, you'll earn the Connector badge. The referral counter and your progress toward the badge are shown right on the Portal page. Like everything else here, it's purely about onboarding and engagement — there is no payout, no points, and no airdrop attached to referrals.

Getting started

Getting set up takes less than a minute:

  1. Go to Oneliq Portal at oneliq.xyz/portal.
  2. Connect your wallet. Any EVM wallet works.
  3. Join the Oneliq Discord and link it from your profile.
  4. Follow Oneliq on X and mark the step complete.
  5. Complete your first check-in — hold the button and sign.
  6. Share your referral link to invite friends and work toward the Connector badge.
  7. Return each day to continue your streak.

Why this matters

Arc is designed as a stablecoin-native settlement layer, and the best way to understand a network is to use it. Oneliq Portal is a simple entry point for anyone who wants to start interacting with Arc without performing swaps, bridging assets, or configuring complex workflows.

A single check-in is a small action. Weeks of consistent participation — and a few friends brought along — add up to real familiarity with the network.

Testnet only

No token. No airdrop. No promises.
Oneliq Portal operates entirely on Arc Testnet (chain ID 5042002) using testnet assets with no monetary value. It is an onboarding and community campaign — there is no token, no points program, no airdrop, and no promise of future rewards. Streaks, badges, and referrals exist to encourage engagement and help you build a history of on-chain activity while exploring Arc. Never send mainnet USDC to a testnet address; funds sent to a testnet address are unrecoverable.
