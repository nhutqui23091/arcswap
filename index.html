◇ Getting Started

From Wallet to Yield
in Four Steps

Connect, fund with testnet USDC, deposit into the USYC vault, then trade or LP whenever you want — your stablecoins keep earning between actions.

Step 01
🦊
Connect Wallet

Use MetaMask or any EVM-compatible wallet. Add Arc Testnet (chainId 5042002) and you're ready — USDC is the native gas token.

Step 02
💧
Get Testnet USDC

Claim free USDC from faucet.circle.com — Circle's official Arc Testnet faucet. The same USDC works for vault, trade, pool, and gas.

Step 03
◇
Deposit into Vault

Open the USYC Vault, mint USYC against your USDC and start earning tokenized U.S. Treasury yield via Hashnote's on-chain Teller.

Step 04
◆
Trade, Bridge or LP

Swap stablecoins inside Arc, bridge USDC across chains via CCTP, or provide liquidity to Arc-native pools — all from the same balance.

A Yield Terminal
for Stablecoins on Arc

ArcSwap is built on Arc — Circle's institutional L1 where USDC is the native gas token. Idle stablecoins earn tokenized U.S. Treasury yield through USYC, while the same balance routes seamlessly through swap, bridge and pool — one wallet, one venue, one stack.
