◇ Getting Started

From Wallet to
Spend Anywhere

Connect, fund with testnet USDC, see your unified balance across chains, then spend instantly anywhere — and let idle stablecoins keep earning USYC yield in the background.

Step 01
🦊
Connect Wallet

Use MetaMask or any EVM-compatible wallet. Add Arc Testnet (chainId 5042002) and you're ready — USDC is the native gas token.

Step 02
💧
Get Testnet USDC

Claim free USDC from faucet.circle.com — Circle's official Arc Testnet faucet. The same USDC works for trade, vault, pool, and gas.

Step 03
◆
Spend Anywhere

Open Trade and pick a source — wallet on any chain or Circle Gateway pre-deposits. CCTP V2 settles your USDC across chains in seconds.

Step 04
◇
Earn While Idle

Park spare USDC in the USYC Vault to earn tokenized U.S. Treasury yield via Hashnote's on-chain Teller. Redeem any market hour.

◈ Roadmap

Where We're Going

A focused build path — unifying stablecoin spend across chains, layering yield on idle balances, then hardening for ecosystem rollout.

Q2 · 2026
◆
Public Beta + Unified Spend

Launch ArcSwap public beta on Arc Testnet. Ship Unified Balance (wallet + Gateway as one selector), production CCTP V2 flows, and polished wallet UX with EIP-6963 detection.

Q3 · 2026
◇
Stablecoin Yield + Onboarding

Integrate the USYC vault with smoother Entitlements (allowlist) UX, redemption queue UI, position history. Add account abstraction for gasless USDC-paid transactions.

Q4 · 2026
◈
Reliability + Ecosystem

Transaction reliability and observability, additional chain support (high-stablecoin-volume networks), spend analytics with B2B memo references, and production hardening for mainnet readiness.

2027 · Beyond
◉
StableFX & Arc Mainnet

RFQ-driven USDC ⇄ EURC PvP settlement via the Arc FxEscrow contract, multi-vault expansion beyond USYC, and Arc Mainnet launch when ready.
