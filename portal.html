FAQ
What is the Oneliq Portal?
The Portal is your home base on Arc Testnet. Check in daily (a real on-chain transaction) to build a streak, finish onboarding tasks, collect badges, invite friends, and climb the leaderboard. It's testnet only — no token, no rewards, just bragging rights.
What are Star Points?
Star Points (★) are a single score that measures how active you are. You earn them from onboarding tasks, badges, referrals, and daily check-ins. Your total is shown at the top and powers the Top Stars leaderboard.
How are Star Points calculated?
Onboarding tasks: Follow on X +20, Join Discord +20, Like +5, Retweet +5, Say GM +5.
Badges: Welcome +50, 7-Day Streak +40, 100 Transactions +150, Connector +200, OG +75.
Referrals: +30 for each friend who finishes onboarding.
Daily check-in: +3 each day you check in.
How do referrals work?
Share your Portal link from the Referral section. When a friend opens it and completes all five onboarding tasks (earning the Welcome badge), they count as an invite — you get +30 Star Points per friend, and reaching 10 unlocks the Connector badge. Invites only count once a friend finishes onboarding, so a click alone isn't enough.
What is the leaderboard?
Tap 🏆 Leaderboard at the top to see two rankings: Top Stars (most Star Points) and Top Invites (most friends invited). To show up with your X username, save your handle in Profile; otherwise a shortened wallet address is shown.
What is a streak and how do freezes work?
Your streak counts consecutive days you check in. Miss a day and a freeze saves your streak automatically (you start with 3, and earn a bonus one at days 7, 30, and 100). Run out of freezes and miss a day, and the streak resets to 1.
