How Unified Balance works
// STEP 1

Deposit

From any supported chain (Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, etc.), deposit USDC into Circle Gateway's vault contract on that chain.

// STEP 2

Aggregate

Gateway tracks deposits across chains and exposes a single chain-agnostic balance you can spend anywhere supported.

// STEP 3

Spend instantly

Spend on Arc (or any supported chain). Gateway routes liquidity in the background — funds arrive at the destination in seconds, not minutes.

// STEP 4

No bridges

Skip CCTP burn-mint cycles, skip wrapped tokens, skip switching networks. One balance, one button.
